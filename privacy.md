## Analytics

This website uses [Plausible Analytics](https://plausible.io), a privacy-friendly analytics tool. Plausible does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and is fully GDPR-compliant. No personal information is shared with third parties.

## Contact data

When you contact EEG101 by email, your email address and message are used solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not share contact data with third parties.

## COST platform (e-COST)

Membership of EEG101 is managed through the [e-COST platform](https://e-services.cost.eu/), operated by COST. Please refer to [COST's privacy policy](https://www.cost.eu/privacy-policy/) for details of how your data is handled in that context.

## Third-party services

This website links to external services including YouTube, GitHub, and Discord. Please refer to those platforms' own privacy policies when using their services.
