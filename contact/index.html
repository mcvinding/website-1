Get in touch with EEG101

Get in touch

We welcome enquiries from researchers, institutions, industry partners, and anyone interested in EEG science and open research.

General enquiries

For general questions about EEG101, the COST Action, or how to get involved:

eeg101@eeg101.eu

Joining the network

See the How to Join page for step-by-step instructions, or join the EEG101 Discord ↗ to connect with the community immediately.

Grant enquiries

For questions about STSMs, VMGs, and Conference Grants, see the Grants page or email the Grant Awarding Coordinator: eeg101@eeg101.eu

Science communication

For podcast, social media, or press enquiries, contact the Science Communication Coordinator via eeg101@eeg101.eu.

Website and content updates

To suggest a correction or update to the website, open an issue on GitHub ↗ or email the coordination team.

Code of Conduct concerns

For Code of Conduct matters, contact the Action Chair or Vice Chair directly and in confidence.
